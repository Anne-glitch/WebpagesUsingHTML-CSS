About the Programme

The Post Graduate Programme in Management (PGP) is a master’s level programme, leading to the grant of a degree of Master of Business Administration (MBA). The two-year full-time residential programme is designed to equip students to take on leadership roles in an increasingly complex and dynamic global scenario. It lasts six trimesters, spread over two years, with a summer project intermediary. PGP is one of the most highly placed programmes in global rankings and is currently ranked 31st in the Financial Times ranking-2022

Admission Criteria

CAT/GMAT score

The cut-off scores may vary from year-to-year. Check all criterias and build up a strong profile to maximise your chances of admission.

Personal Interview

A personal interview is scheduled upon shortlisting of candidates. This step is essential to know the candidate's aptitude and attitude.

Last Step

Final selection is based on the CAT performance, Scholastic Achievement, Work Experience, performance in the Writing Ability Test, and Interview.
